➡ Tiya Sircar Biography

Tiya Sircar is an actress, hails from America. She is best known for her outstanding work and the amazing contribution to the world of cinema. She has acted in many films and gained recognition for her roles that she played very beautifully and won the hearts of millions of her fans around the world. 8 Things You Didn’t Know About Tiya Sircar

Her Birth & Family Background

She was born on 16th May 1982, America. Her parents originally belong to Calcutta, India and both are working are professors in college. She is of Indian descent.

Her Educational Background

She got her education from the University of Texas, Austin where she honored two bachelor’s degrees in Bussiness/Marketing & another one in Theatre & Dance. Later, she moved to Los Angeles to pursue her passion for acting.

She Began Dancing At Young Age

Her parents instilled her the love for arts and she began dancing at the age of 3 and learned Indian classical & folk dance, ballet, jazz, lyrical & modern forms of dance.

She Is Best Known For

She is best known for her roles in “The Internship”, “17 Again”, and “The Good Place”.

She Is Fluent In Bengali

She is a multilingual as she can speak Hindi & English but also fluent in Bengali as well.

She Was Featured In Magazine

She was featured in Anokhi MAgazine’s “Sexy and Successful in 2010”  full-page coverage in their 7th Anniversary issue. 

Her Television Career

She appeared in many television serials included “Master Of None”, “House MD”, “Hannah” and so many popular series that gave her extraordinary recognition for her amazing work.

She Voiced For Disney Show

In 2008, she voiced for an animated character for the Disney show named “Phineas & Ferb”.
