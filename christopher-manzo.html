Christopher Thomas Manzo, the Manzo family’s newest member, was born in New Jersey on May 25, 1989, to TV personality Caroline Manzo and Albert Manzo. He is a reality television star who is most known for being Caroline Manzo’s younger son on RHONJ.

Christopher Thomas is an author and a businessman who owns a restaurant in New Jersey. He has appeared in films such as Manzo’d with Children and The Millionaire Matchmaker with his family. Chris grew up with his brother Albie Manzo, who has always been very close to him, and the two even co-host a podcast.
