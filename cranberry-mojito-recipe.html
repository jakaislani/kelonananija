    Cranberry Mojitos

    These cranberry mojitos are perfect for any night but especially a celebration! 

    Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword cranberry mojitos, mojito Prep Time 5 minutes minutes 5 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 148kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 cup white rum
    • 1/2 cup cranberries
    • 1/4 cup mint leaves
    • 1 lime cut into wedges
    • 1/2 cup sweet & sour
    • 3/4 cup cranberry juice
    • 3/4 cup lemon-lime soda

    Instructions

    • Muddle together the rum, cranberries, mint, and lime. Strain.

    • Stir in sweet & sour, lemon-lime soda, and cranberry juice.

    • Serve over ice. Garnish with additional fresh cranberries, mint leaves and lime slices.
