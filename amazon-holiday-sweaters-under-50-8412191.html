8 Under-$50 Holiday Sweaters to Wear to All of Your Winter Gatherings

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Sweaters basically go hand-in-hand with the holidays, and if you’re like me, you’re on the hunt for the perfect option for all of your festive plans. Because let’s face it, there’s nothing more comfortable (or cuter) than a warm, snuggly top whether you opt for a turtleneck, cable-knit pick, cardigan, or anything else in between. If that sounds like your dream winter getup, then keep reading for eight under-$50 festive sweaters that you’ll be able to wear on Christmas day, New Year’s Eve, and beyond. 

Under-$50 Holiday Sweaters

Dokotoo Chunky Cable-Knit Sweater

For a tasteful dose of holiday cheer, consider the Dokotoo cable-knit sweater. Featuring balloon sleeves, cinched wrists, a ribbed waist, and a round neckline, this separate is full of texture and playful elements. One shopper called it a “treasured sweater,” claiming the cable stitching and sleeves to be “beautiful” and “stunning.” “Do I like [the sweater]?” asked the same reviewer. “Nope, I love it.”  

LookbookStore Holiday Knit Sweater

If you’re feeling extra festive, the Lookbookstore holiday sweater may be for you. It offers a classic Fair Isle-style design, combining cool-toned colors like white, gray, and muted gold. The longer ribbed sleeves can be left as is to keep your hands extra toasty, or roll them up to create a sleek cuff. The waist’s height is perfect for tucking into jeans, while the relaxed fit offers various layering capabilities. Best of all, it’s quirky enough to wear to Christmas sweater parties, yet chic enough to “wear after Christmas due to the gray color and snowflake design,” according to a shopper.

Newshows Ribbed Button Sweater

Opt for a fitted look with Newshows ribbed sweater. Available in sizes S to XXL, this pick includes a scoop neckline, form-fitting silhouette, longer hemline, button-up details, and a split sleeve. It’s a closet staple that you’re sure to wear more than once since you can seamlessly dress it up or down. Elevate it with a mini skirt, sheer tights, and knee-high boots, or dress it down with jeans and sneakers. Even better, one shopper calls it "comfortable and flattering,” naming it a “perfect combination.”

Zesica V-Neck Belted Sweater Top

I don’t know about you, but this Zesica sweater top instantly reminds me of a wrapped Christmas present — and that’s everything. Look forward to a deep V-neckline, sweet off-the-shoulder moment, ruffled hem, tie-front bow, and shimmery fabric. It’s pretty and feminine, embracing a flirty, fun aesthetic. “It is super flattering,” said a shopper. “I am a size 14 and the tie sits at the smallest part of my waist giving me an hourglass shape! It is a great sweater for work and date nights!”
