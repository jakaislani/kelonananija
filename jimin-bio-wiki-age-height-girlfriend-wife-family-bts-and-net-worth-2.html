Jimin BTS

Ji Min is a vastly recognized pop singer, lead vocalist, producer, and dancer for the South Korean boy band Bangtan Boys, or BTS. He made his debut in 2013 as a member of BTS with the release of the single “No More Dream”.

It is known that Park and his group have put out albums such as Dark & Wild and Wake Up. Afterward, they became the first Kpop group to win a Billboard award in 2017. During this time, they beat out Justin Bieber and Ariana Grande.

Most noteworthy, Ji Min released two solo tracks, “Lie” and “Serendipity” under BTS in the album Wings. On the other hand, “Serendipity” from the album Love Yourself: Answer was released in 2018.

Citing their work, Park’s band was awarded the fifth-class Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit in 2018 by the South Korean President. He collaborated with Jungkook on two songs, “Christmas Day” and “we don’t Talk Anymore”.
